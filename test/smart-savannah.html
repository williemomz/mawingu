    • SMART SAVANNAH

    Smart Savannah builds digital solutions for non-metropolitan Africa. The team is comprised of professionals with skills and experience in software development, UI/UX design, sales, product and project management.

    Smart Savannah's main focus is developing IoT solutions to help businesses prosper. Our flagship product, BloomCare, is already serving horticultural farms in Kenya by helping them to manage pests, diseases & staff productivity. We are also providing IoT solutions in the wildlife conservation space by helping to track wildlife and reduce poaching as well as provide guided tours and remote viewing of the wildlife conservancies through a live stream feature.

    We collaborate with Microsoft 4Afrika to assist with the upskilling of young talented developers through an internship program that gives them marketable software skills. We have successfully trained and assisted more than 20 individuals to get industry jobs through this program within our first 12 months of running it.
