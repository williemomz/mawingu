    Mawingu is an internet access provider serving homes, businesses and public hotspots (WiFi Zones) across Kenya in areas with poor infrastructure. Microsoft has invested in the company and supports its operations. Mawingu also offers digital products and IoT solutions.

    A hotspot is a specific area where you can connect your WiFi enabled device to the Internet. Customers can enjoy Internet by visiting these hotspots, using a one-time login (access code).

    There are hundreds of hotspots across Kenya. Download the Mawingu app from the Google Play Store to find the nearest one. Every hotspot is supported locally by a Mawingu agent to help you get online.

    Your access to the world has no limits. Mawingu provides broadband internet suitable for high-res video streaming and data-heavy applications.
